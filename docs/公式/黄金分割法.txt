输入：单谷函数f,初始区间[a_0, b_0],精度ε
输出：极小值点所在的区间[a, b]

(1)令 k=0, ρ=(3-sqrt(5))/2
(2)l_k=b_k-a_k, 若 l_k<ε, 输出 [a, b] = [a_k, b_k]
(3)a_try=a_k+ρ*l_k, b_try=b_k-ρ*l_k
(4)若 f(a_try)>f(b_try), 则 a_(k+1)=a_try, b_(k+1)=b_k；
若 f(a_try)<f(b_try), 则 a_(k+1)=a_k, b_(k+1)=b_try；
若 f(a_try)=f(b_try), 则 a_(k+1)=a_try, b_(k+1)=b_try
(5)k=k+1, 转 (2)
