需求函数拟合 \( D(p) = \beta p + \alpha \)

推导过程:
1. 无偏估计: \( E(y) = \beta E(x) + \alpha \implies \bar{y} = \beta \bar{x} + \alpha \)
2. 消除变量 \( \alpha \): \( \alpha = \bar{y} - \beta \bar{x} \)
3. 优化目标: \( \min L = \sum (y_i - (\beta x_i + \alpha))^2 = \beta^2 \sum (x_i - \bar{x})^2 - 2\beta \sum (y_i - \bar{y})(x_i - \bar{x}) + \text{常数} \)
